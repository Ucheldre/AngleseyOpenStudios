Oriel Môn

Oriel Môn yw prif amgueddfa ac Oriel arddangos Ynys Môn.
I’w chynulleidfa artistig, mae’r Oriel yn cynnig rhaglen ddeinamig o arddangosfeydd creadigol o bob math o ffurfiau celf,
gan arddangos gwaith arlunwyr profiadol a thalentau ifanc fel ei gilydd.

Oriel Môn is Anglesey’s premier purpose built museum and art gallery.
For its artistic audience, the Oriel offers a dynamic programme of creative exhibitions from all
art forms, displaying the works of established artists and young talents alike.
