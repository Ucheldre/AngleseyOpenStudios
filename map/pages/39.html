Christine Williams

Rwy’n artist cyfrwng cymysg sy’n gweithio gyda gwydr môr, broc môr, ffeltio nodwydd, gwaith gwifren a papur ond yn ddiweddar rwyf hefyd wedi dod o hyd i gariad at waith gwydr.
Rwy’n gweld gwydr mewn golau hollol wahanol nawr.

I am a mixed media artist working with sea glass, driftwood, needle felting, wirework, paper, but I’ve recently also found a love for glass work, so i break and crush glass and carefully cut it all with nippers.
I see glass in a whole different light now.
